Our Story

Born in Mardan,
made for everyone

Aladdin Cafe & Cream started with one simple belief: great food doesn't need to cost a fortune. We set up shop in Mardan to give the city a place that feels premium without being pretentious.

Every burger is hand-pressed, every shake hand-blended, and every scoop served with a bit of theatre. We're not a chain — we're yours.
